Our Products

Our products have been created by a few of the many gifted artists from the Arts of Life. Arts of Life is a wonderful non–profit organization that employs mentally challenged artists that gives every body an opportunity to grow. For every t–shirt you buy, a portion of the money will go back to this awesome institution and the very artist who created the art for your mind–boggling tee.

About Us

We Are Lions wants to give you the opportunity to rock some shirts never seen before. Every time you wear a roaring shirt, you give these artists a window to spread their wings and travel across the world. We Are Lion is a walking advertisement for not only an extraordinary person, but for yourself. With groundbreaking technology, wear a mural, a comic, and a story. Help make a beautiful painting slide off the wall and pulsate. Our goal is to reach out to as many special artists and organizations as humanly possible to create double–take apparel and give as much money back to the people who create these outstanding pieces of art.

Blue Brother- By: Jean Wilson

Artist’s Statement

Art is relaxing. It makes me feel happy, and it makes me feel proud.

I prefer to make animals in my artwork, because I like animals. Dogs are my favorite. Paint is my favorite medium to work with. I am learning how to mix colors. First I draw outlines, then I paint. Sometimes on windows and sometimes on paper. I do collage too.

What I see in my artwork is my life. The painting of L’Arche house, where I live, is my favorite, because I learned something new when I made it. I get my ideas from my head and also from my life and family.

Doggie Days- By: Gaby Carroll

Artist’s Statement

I’ve been doing art since I was a little but I didn’t know I was this good until I came to work here and I love my job!

I paint on windows. I like painting on the glass.

My Coral scene was my first window and I couldn’t think of anything to do with that window and I started making a design on it and it just looked like coral.

My Jerusalem painting was inspired by a trip to Israel in ’06.

My Grandma painting is inspired by Grandma. She’d been sick for a long time and during my try-out days at Arts of Life, she showed me the painting and brushes and everything, and I miss her.

Everything is coming up roses. Men go moon- By: David Krueger

"David Krueger combines bold colors and symbolic motifs. His geometric style is characterized by horizontal bands drawn across the canvas, which break up the frame into narrative sections, reminiscent of a comic book. Between these lines the artist uses decorative motifs including stars, zig-zags, squares, crosses, and x’s. Next to and within the abstract elements, David creates stylized depictions of humans and animals. The structures that he builds, and repetitive elements that he uses, make a wonderful stylistic complement to the fantastic imagery that flows freely from his imagination." - Arts of Life

About Us

We Are Lions wants to give you the opportunity to rock some shirts never seen before. Every time you wear a roaring shirt, you give these artists a window to spread their wings and travel across the world. We Are Lion is a walking advertisement for not only an extraordinary person, but for yourself. With groundbreaking technology, wear a mural, a comic, and a story. Help make a beautiful painting slide off the wall and pulsate. Our goal is to reach out to as many special artists and organizations as humanly possible to create double–take apparel and give as much money back to the people who create these outstanding pieces of art.
